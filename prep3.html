Creamy Chocolate Fudge

Description

This creamy chocolate fudge is rich, smooth, and melts in your mouth.

Perfect for holidays, parties, or whenever you're craving something sweet and chocolatey.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2/3 cup evaporated milk
  • 1 package (12 oz) semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Combine sugar, butter, and milk in a saucepan. Bring to a boil for 5 minutes.
  2. Remove from heat and stir in chocolate chips and vanilla until smooth.
  3. Pour into a greased pan and refrigerate until firm. Cut into squares.
